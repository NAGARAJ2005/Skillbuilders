Course Upload Instructions

Important Instructions for Course Submission

Follow the instructions below to upload your course to Skill Builders. Ensure the information provided is accurate, as errors could lead to your course being rejected.

1. Course Title

Enter a clear and concise title for your course. Avoid special characters or overly long titles. It should accurately describe the course content.

2. Course Description

The course description should be detailed, explaining what the course will cover, the skills students will gain, and any special features. Proofread for spelling or grammatical errors before submitting.

3. Course Duration

Enter the total duration of the course in hours (e.g., 10, 15, or 30 hours). Make sure the duration accurately reflects the course's length.

4. Number of Modules

Specify the number of modules that make up the course. The number should be a whole number (e.g., 5, 12). Ensure that the number matches the actual number of modules in the course.

5. Course Thumbnail

Provide a relevant image for the course thumbnail. It should be a high-quality image that represents the course content. Upload the correct file path or URL for the image.

6. Course Price

For paid courses, enter the price in numerical format (e.g., 19.99). For free courses, leave this field empty or enter "0". Ensure the price is accurate before submitting.

7. Select Related Streams

Choose the streams that best match the course content (e.g., Web Development, Data Science, etc.). Hold down the "Ctrl" (or "Command" on Mac) key to select multiple streams.

8. Course Prerequisites

If your course has prerequisites (e.g., prior knowledge of a programming language), list them here. Separate multiple prerequisites with commas. This field helps set the expectations for students before they take the course.

General Tips

  • Ensure all fields are filled out completely and accurately.
  • Double-check the details before submitting. Any errors may cause delays or rejection of your course.
  • If you face any issues during the submission process, contact our support team for assistance.

Submission Review

Your course submission will be reviewed by the admin team. You will receive feedback regarding its approval or if any modifications are required. Stay updated by visiting your personal page where you can view the status of your uploaded courses.
